About Us

Our Story

Welcome to The Gospel Study Center (TGSC), a haven for spiritual growth and emotional well-being. TGSC through the Glory of God was Founded by Chaplain Shobayo Kayode. TGSC is a dedicated center for profound Bible study and compassionate counseling.

Our vision is to be a center for gospel-centered counseling and biblical study, impacting lives, families, and communities. We envision a world where individuals experience the fullness of God's love and find restoration in both their spiritual and emotional well-being.

Founder: Chaplain Shobayo Kayode

Chaplain Shobayo Kayode, the visionary behind TGSC, is an ordained servant of God with a passion for guiding individuals on their spiritual journey. With years of pastoral experience and biblical counseling expertise,Chaplain Kayode has played a vital role through the help of God in transforming lives and strengthening faith.

Our Mission

At TGSC, our mission is twofold: to offer expert biblical counseling services and to foster spiritual growth through in-depth study of the word of God. We believe that by combining the transformative power of counseling with the wisdom found in the Scriptures, individuals can experience holistic healing and achieve a deeper understanding of their faith.

What Sets the Center Apart

  1. Biblical Counseling: Our counseling services are rooted in the principles of the Bible. We provide a safe and supportive environment for individuals seeking guidance, encouragement, and healing based on biblical truths.
  2. In-Depth Study of God's Word: TGSC is committed to facilitating a profound understanding of the Bible. Through engaging study sessions, seminars, and workshops, we empower our community to apply biblical principles to their daily lives.
